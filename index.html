Agenda

  1. Introduction: who/what is Publishing@W3C? (Tzviya Siegman)

  4. Accessibility and the European Accessibility Act (Avneesh Singh)
  5. How can people participate, and keep up with our activities (Mateus Teixeira)
  6. Q & A (moderated by Tzviya Siegman)
