Publishing@W3C Community Webinar

The webinars are available at two times to accommodate global attendance:

  • July 27, 2020, 15:00 UTC
  • July 29, 2020, 00:00 UTC (July 28 in the Americas)

Come join us to hear from publishing industry experts from the US, Europe and Asia, and to learn the impressive results of the global publishing survey developed by the W3C Publishing Business Group. Understand how the Publishing@W3C community is using these results to move forward and to engage your participation in many important new areas of standards work.

Agenda

  1. Introduction: who/what is Publishing@W3C? (Tzviya Siegman)
  2. Overview of the goals of renewed Publishing@W3C activity (Cristina Mussinelli)
  3. EPUB Survey Overview and its influence on the future of the Publishing@W3C activity (Wendy Reid)
  4. Accessibility and the European Accessibility Act (Avneesh Singh)
  5. How can people participate, and keep up with our activities (Mateus Teixeira)
  6. Q & A (moderated by Tzviya Siegman, Daihei Shiohama (塩濱大平), and Shinya Takami (高見真也))
